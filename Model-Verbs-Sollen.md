The German modal verb sollen (meaning "should", "ought to", or "be supposed to") is conjugated irregularly in the present tense.

It implies an external command, obligation, or hearsay.


***Present Tense (Präsens)***
* ich soll - I should
 
* do sollst - You should

* er/es/sie soll - he/it/she should

* wir sollen - We should

* ihr sollt - You should

* sie/Sie sollen - They/ You formal should


***Preterite (Past - "should"):***
* ich sollte

* du solltest

* er/es/sie sollte

* wir sollten

* ihr solltet

* sie/Sie sollten


***Perfect (Past - "have been supposed to"):***
ich habe gesollt - I should have

du hast gesollt - You shoukd have


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

***Key Usage examples:***

* Du sollst dein Zimmer aufräumen - You are supposed to clean your room - (external order)

* Was soll ich machen ? - What should I do?

* Er soll sechr reich sein - He is said to be very rich -( hearsay/rumor)


* du sollst jetzt noch Huase gehen. - You should go home now.

* Der Film soll gut sein. - The film is supposed to be good.


* Was soll ich tun?  - What should I do?

* wo soll ich denn schlafen? - Where should I sleep?
